공기역학적 형태 최적화 (Aerodynamic Form Optimization)

We help you translate design concepts into wind-friendly forms. Our aerodynamics studies yield an external form that is fully realizable in construction, yet still true to your vision. By making ideas buildable, these studies empower ambitious and innovative design.

RWDI는 귀하가 다음을 추구할 때 가치 있는 파트너가 되어드립니다

01

혁신의 탐구

Improve the likelihood of faithfully realizing a challenging original concept.

02

기회의 창출

Identify early the form features that will later drive structural design costs.

03

도전 과제 해결

Quickly analyze trade-offs between form and myriad structural concerns.

04

기대 충족

Produce quantified design options in a fast-moving context.


Deliver a demonstrably buildable design.

디자인 실현

비전 있는 디자인 실현

Our wind-tunnel studies have guided the early design of several highly recognizable buildings. For example, the gentle twist of the Shanghai Tower distributes the maximum cladding loads while lowering costs and maintaining the desired aesthetic.

최적화 이점

A project refined through wind studies will be...

  • More in tune with the elements
  • Better suited to its location in the world
  • More effective in its use of materials
  • Less expensive to build
  • More cost efficient to manage long term
