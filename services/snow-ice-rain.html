적설, 결빙 및 강우 (Snow, Ice & Rain)

We help you manage precipitation in all its forms and consequences: Snow as it drifts, accumulates, freezes and melts; Rain as it drifts into or accumulates around a structure; Ice as it forms or melts. The physics of all these states and transformations is challenging. We take account of detailed technical factors without losing sight of your big picture.

Services
Falling snow and ice

We quantify risk over a return period, much like the standard practice for wind loading risks to structures or cladding. We consider a complex set of site- and project-specific factors that 'age' snow and ice and contribute to the likelihood that they will release and fall.

Snow loading

We provide fine-tuned snow loading values that are customized to your building or structure and its site. With these values you can make optimum choices for form and materials.

Wind-blown snow

We use local wind patterns and meteorology to predict how snow particles will be picked up, carried and dropped by the wind near your structure. With this data we pinpoint changes you can make to prevent undesirable accumulations.

Wind-driven rain

Wind patterns around a structure can carry rain to unexpected and undesirable places—both outside and inside the structure. We help you spot and avoid such problems.
