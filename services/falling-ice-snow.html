낙설 및 낙빙 (Falling Snow & Ice)

We help you manage the risk of harmful consequences from the accumulation and release of ice and snow. These two processes are governed by many complex factors. With our advanced analysis, you can be confident ice and snow risks have been anticipated, so they can be correctly addressed.

위험 관리

적설 및 노화 모델링

Working with the details of your building and location, we consider how snow will accumulate and 'age.' Factors we model for aging include air temperature, solar radiation, wind forces, building heat loss, insulation properties of accumulated snow, daily freezing/thawing, surface friction of building materials, and melt-water drainage paths.
