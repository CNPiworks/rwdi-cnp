환경 성능 분석

일조 분석 (Solar Studies)

We quantify how buildings interact with sunlight. By learning in detail how your project responds to the sun, you can: Improve operating efficiency; Improve occupant comfort; Avoid (or mitigate) dangerous or adverse conditions, such as glare and shadow; Optimize your strategies for solar power generation.

혁신의 탐구

Design for high performance through early, unbiased assessment of energy needs and system sizing.

Present exceptional, meticulously considered spaces to premium clientele.

03

도전 과제 해결
