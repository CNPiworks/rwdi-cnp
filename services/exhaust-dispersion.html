배기 확산 및 설계 (Exhaust Dispersion & Design)

[cite_start]We help you manage building air quality to enhance occupant experience, and improve building and equipment performance. The effect of air quality in and around a building can make or break a project. Exhaust travels with the wind, which means it can end up where least expected. It takes a specialist's eye to bring effective, practical solutions to this often-complicated issue. [cite: 267-270]
