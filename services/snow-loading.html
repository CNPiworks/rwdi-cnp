설하중 (Snow Loading)

Accumulated snow can severely impact built environment projects of any type. We help you plan for and manage these effects. Getting snow loading right at the design stage lowers both costs and risks. We provide fine-tuned snow loading values customized to your building or structure.

적설 패턴 테스트

RWDI frequently uses physical models to test snow accumulation patterns, particularly if the design is complex. Since the physics of snow and wind are similar to those of sand and water, we use special sand in a water flume to simulate blizzards.
